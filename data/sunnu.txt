Money is a type of measure.
Units of Money are Cents, Pennies, Quarters, Dollars, Dimes and Nickels.
One Pennie is one Cent.
Five Pennies is a Nickel.
2 Nickels is a Dime.
25 Cents is a Quarter.
4 Quarters is a Dollar.
